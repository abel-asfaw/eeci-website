# ✝️ EECI Church Website

**Ethio Emmanuel Church International – Official Website Revamp Project**

---

## 📌 Purpose

To redesign and modernize the Ethio Emmanuel Church website to better reflect our mission, serve new visitors, and support our church community online.

---

## ❗ Problem Statement

The current website feels outdated, has inconsistencies in design, and lacks clarity in content structure. New visitors may feel confused or miss important updates due to cluttered sections, outdated content, or low visual hierarchy.

---

## 👥 Target Audience

- First-time visitors looking to learn about our church
- Church members seeking announcements, livestreams, or contact info
- Volunteers managing church news, media, or events

---

## 🎯 Objectives

### ✅ Goals
- Improve first-time visitor experience
- Keep events and videos up-to-date
- Modernize the homepage design with better visual flow
- Centralize and streamline content management (via WordPress)
- Enable team collaboration with GitHub and project tools

### ❌ Non-goals
- Building a completely custom CMS
- Supporting non-WordPress hosting platforms for now

---

## 🏗️ Current Status

- GitHub repository initialized ✅
- WordPress chosen for CMS ✅
- Project planning started on ClickUp ✅
- Selecting a new theme and reviewing what to keep or change on the site ✅
